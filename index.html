Call Break Rules

Game Overview

Call Break is a trick-taking card game for 4 players. The objective is to accurately predict and win the number of tricks you bid.

Bidding Phase

  • Each player receives 13 cards
  • Players bid how many tricks they think they'll win (0-13)
  • The total bids cannot equal 13
  • Spades are always trump

Playing Phase

  • The player who won the previous trick leads the next one
  • Players must follow suit if possible
  • If you can't follow suit, you may play any card
  • The highest card of the led suit wins, unless a trump (spade) is played
  • The highest spade always wins the trick

Scoring

  • If you make your exact bid: +10 points + bid amount
  • For each overtrick: +1 point per trick
  • For each undertrick: -1 point per trick
  • Game continues for multiple rounds
